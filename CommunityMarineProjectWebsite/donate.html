Support Our Ocean

Together, we can protect our marine ecosystems and empower coastal communities. Your gift makes a direct impact in sustaining coral reefs, mangroves, fisheries, and the people who depend on them.

How Your Donation Helps

Marine Advocacy & Awareness

Help us amplify community voices in protecting marine resources and influence better policies for our oceans.

LMMA Management

Provide rangers with tools, equipment, patrol boats, and fuel to safeguard our marine areas from illegal activities.

Mangrove & Coral Restoration

Support hands-on habitat restoration that rebuilds fish populations, protects our shores, and strengthens climate resilience.

Environmental Education

Bring conservation programs into schools, inspiring the next generation of ocean guardians.
