About Patte Conservation

Our Purpose

The purpose of Patte Conservation is to improve the quality of life of its community members through undertaking developmental projects and programs on environmental conservation initiatives.

We intend to establish a Locally Managed Marine Area (LMMA) to foster a sustainable and equitable marine ecosystem that benefits both the environment and local communities.

Through collaborative management by the community of Pate, Shanga Ishakani and Shanga Rubu, we preserve coral reef health, promote biodiversity, and support sustainable livelihoods.

Food Security

Contributing to sustainable food sources through responsible marine resource management

Economic Development

Supporting sustainable livelihoods and community well-being through eco-tourism

Marine Conservation

Preserving coral reef health and promoting marine biodiversity

Sustainable Resources

Ensuring long-term viability of marine resources for future generations
