Mission

To empower the communities of Patte, Shanga Ishakani, and Shanga Rubu to conserve and sustainably manage marine and coastal resources through community-led initiatives, environmental education, coral restoration, ecotourism, and the establishment of Locally Managed Marine Areas (LMMA).

Vision

A resilient and thriving Patte Island community living in harmony with a restored and sustainably managed marine and coastal ecosystem.
