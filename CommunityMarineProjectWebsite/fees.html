Other Activities
Research KES 10,000 (plus conservation fees)
Filming (less than 10 crews) KES 20,000 (plus conservation fees)
Filming (more than 10 crews) KES 30,000 (plus conservation fees)
  • Research: Researchers must pay a flat fee of KES 10,000 and also adhere to the standard conservation fees. They are required to submit preliminary findings within one day of concluding their research and provide a full report within three months.
  • Filming: Filming crews will pay a fee based on the number of crew members. The fee includes a base rate and the standard conservation fees determined by the management committee as illustrated from the table above.
  • Annual Review: The conservation fees will be reviewed annually by the management committee to ensure they remain appropriate and sustainable.

Conservation Guidelines

Marine Protected Area Rules

  • No fishing or collection of marine life
  • Do not touch or stand on coral reefs
  • Use reef-safe sunscreen only
  • Follow designated swimming/diving areas
  • Maintain respectful distance from marine life
  • No littering - take only photos, leave only bubbles

What to Bring

  • Valid identification document
  • Payment receipt (show to rangers)
  • Reef-safe sunscreen
  • Towel and change of clothes
  • Waterproof camera (optional)
  • Drinking water

Payment Confirmation

After successful payment, you'll receive a digital receipt that must be shown to rangers when accessing the marine protected areas. This system helps us track conservation impact and ensure sustainable tourism practices.
