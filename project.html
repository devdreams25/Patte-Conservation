Projects

Our Project

Protecting marine ecosystems through community-driven conservation initiatives

Ongoing Projects

Coral Restoration

In partnership with The Ocean Trust Organization, our community actively restores coral reefs through hands-on restoration activities, sharing skills, knowledge, and tools.

Awareness Campaigns

Educational campaigns focused on environmental conservation, sustainable fishing practices, and the importance of marine ecosystem protection.

Eco-Tourism

Sustainable eco-tourism activities including snorkeling, diving, and sea adventures that support local communities while protecting marine environments.

Future Plans

Marine Life Advocacy

Expanding our advocacy efforts to protect marine life and promote sustainable fishing practices throughout the region.

  • Policy advocacy for marine protection
  • Community education programs
  • Collaboration with government agencies

Mangrove & Seaweed Planting

Large-scale mangrove restoration and seaweed farming initiatives to strengthen coastal protection and create sustainable income sources for local communities.

Coral Restoration Partnership

In partnership with The Ocean Trust Organization, our community has taken an active role in coral reef restoration efforts.

Through this collaboration:

  • Local community members are directly engaged in restoration activities
  • Skills, knowledge, and tools are shared to empower people in marine conservation
  • Together, we are helping rebuild vibrant coral ecosystems that support biodiversity and livelihoods

Patte Conservation

Iweni Locally Managed Marine Area - Fostering sustainable and equitable marine ecosystems that benefit both the environment and local communities through collaborative management by the communities of Pate, Shanga Ishakani and Shanga Rubu.
